## Usage
- Cite the **lead credit** for the prospect's primary genre; the proof-line is "we did the audio on [credit]."
- In an **overflow / co-dev** pitch (the studio has audio), the credit shows scale you can match, not a gap you fill.
- **Marvel Snap** doubles as the *funded-mobile precedent* — a mobile studio with budget is not disqualified (`scoring-rubric.md`).
- No close genre match → lead with the audio gap or the catalyst; never force an off-genre credit.
